The goal of this course is to teach the fundamentals of dancing Salsa to complete beginners. By the end of the course, students will be able to join Salsa socials, dance and enjoy them. The course has a strong focus on learning to lead and to follow, which is essential to dancing with new-to-you dancers in a social setting. The contents also include theory of Salsa music and dance, social dancing etiquette and Salsa dance moves. The main style will be Cuban, with some notes of other styles.
